Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that includes noodles, multiple types of cheeses, beef, and tomato sauce.

Lasagna is the food of miracles.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Beef
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles.
  2. Cook the beef.
  3. Mix the beef and tomato sauce.
  4. Start the layers.
  5. Cover and cook at 300 degrees for 45 minutes.
  6. Let cool for 15 minutes.
  7. Serve!
